1.get all article
curl -GET "http://127.0.0.1:5000/article"

2.Get latest n article
curl -GET "http://127.0.0.1:5000/article?limit=2"

3.Get article by title
curl -GET "http://127.0.0.1:5000/article/find?title="yyy""

4.Get article from url(url+article)
curl -GET "http://127.0.0.1:5000/article/8"

5.Insert new article
curl http://127.0.0.1:5000/article -d  '{"title" : "yyy","content" : "once four ","author" : "masu","URL":"http://127.0.0.1:5000/article/7"}'

6.Update content of article
curl -X PATCH --data '{"content": "edited","article_id":8}' http://127.0.0.1:5000/article

7.delete article
curl  -X "DELETE" http://127.0.0.1:5000/article?article_id=7
